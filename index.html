One of the things that interest me the most are UAVs, or drones, as they are commonly known. A UAV (unmanned aerial vehicle) is an aircraft without a human pilot. UAVs have many uses such as military, recreational, agricultural, scientific, and more. They are controlled either by a human operator with a remote control or by a computer. Small UAVs mostly use li-po batteries while larger ones rely on airplane engines. Currently, quadcopters are the most popular civil UAVs. They have a very limited speed and a very short battery life (around 10 minutes of flight time). An idea to improve them is to integrate a wing into the design, in a way that enables the copter to hover, but also to fly horizontally (using lift force). This integration will allow it to operate very efficiently, travel very quickly, very quietly, very smoothly, and for a longer time.
